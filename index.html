Harley's Mission

Harley prefers to play the majestic game of cricket with aggression and fairness. With skilled bowlers and batsman, Harley's team focuses on the fundamentals of the game. With spinners copying the great Shane Warne and batters who play with the technique of Ricky Ponting, Harley's team is aiming for great things this season. Playing in the 5th division currently, this seasons goal is to be promoted to the 4th division.

Why Join Us

The team is full of young aspiring crickets with a hunger to win games and improve on their skills. Joining Harley's Team is more than just cricket, it is a family. All ages are welcome to training and with coaches for every level of experience, we will be more than happy to welcome you to the family. Training is all about improving with net sessions and full ground practice, you will be ready to face any situation come game day.

About Harley

Harley learnt how to play cricket in the nets with his friend. That friend was the best spin bowler in the state and passed on his knowledge to Harley. It was this 'trail by fire' approach that Harley used to teach all grades of cricket today. Harley believes that practice makes perfect and thus training sessions will be full of net hitting and bowling, fielding and game stradegy. With a combination of stadegy and net sessions, Harley's Cricket Team is aiming for a great year. At 28 years of Age, Harley can connect well with our junior players as well as be in good standing with our senior team. Harley has a cooking background so you can expect great BBQ's after games.
